Nymeyer Carpentry is a family owned business dedicated to helping our customers create style, improve organization, and add character to their homes and businesses. All of our builds are constructed individually and by hand, ensuring the highest level of quality and craftsmanship. Each piece is customized to meet our customers' needs. Call for a free consultation.

How we do it

Consultation

The first step is to contact us by email or phone. Have a basic description of the project. We will schedule a time to meet to discuss your style, needs, and ideas regarding the project. We will also take preliminary measurements.

Drawings

The second step is to have the customer gather inspiration photos that can be used in the design of the piece.

We will turn the measurements, customer needs, and inspiration photos into a 3D drawing. The drawings are then emailed to the customer along with a preliminary quote. After reviewing the drawings we will discuss and implement any changes that should be made.

Construction

Once the design is approved by the customer, we begin the build. All builds are hand crafted and built individually to ensure the highest level of craftsmanship.

Installation

Once the build is complete we will contact the customer to schedule a delivery and installation date.
